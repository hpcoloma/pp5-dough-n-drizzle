FAQ's

  1. What types of products do you offer?

    We offer a variety of delicious baked goods, including cakes, cookies, pastries, and custom desserts. All our products are made with high-quality ingredients and can be tailored to fit your specific needs.

  2. How can I place an order?

    You can place an order through our website by visiting the product pages and adding items to your cart. Once you’re ready, proceed to checkout, fill in your details, and complete your purchase. Alternatively, you can also call us to place an order over the phone.

  3. Do you offer delivery?

    Yes, we offer delivery services for your convenience. Estimated delivery time is between 7-10 days. Please check our delivery areas to ensure we can deliver to your location.

  4. Can I pick up my order instead of having it delivered?

    Absolutely! We offer a convenient pick-up option. You can contact us after successful checkout and choose a time that works for you. Our location details will be provided in your confirmation email.

  5. What is your return policy?

    Due to the nature of our products, we do not accept returns. However, if there is an issue with your order or if the product is damaged upon arrival, please contact us within 24 hours, and we will do our best to resolve the issue.

  6. How should I store my baked goods?

    To maintain freshness, store your baked goods in a cool, dry place. For items like cakes and pastries, we recommend keeping them in an airtight container. If your products need refrigeration, please refer to the storage instructions provided with your order.

  7. Do you offer custom cakes?

    Yes, we specialize in custom cakes for all occasions, including birthdays, weddings, and corporate events. Please contact us directly to discuss your design ideas and requirements.

  8. What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment options during the checkout process.

  9. Do you have allergen information?

    Yes, we take food allergies seriously. All our products are labeled with allergen information. If you have specific dietary requirements or allergies, please contact us, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

  10. How can I contact customer service?

    You can reach our customer service team by email at doughndrizzle@gmail.com or by phone at [Your Phone Number]. We’re here to help!

Still Have Questions?

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We’re here to make your experience as sweet as our treats!
